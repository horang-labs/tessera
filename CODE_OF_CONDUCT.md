# Code of Conduct

Tessera contributors are expected to keep project spaces respectful,
constructive, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and considerate.
- Assume good intent while asking for clarity when needed.
- Keep technical disagreement tied to concrete tradeoffs.
- Respect maintainer decisions about scope and timing.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing someone else's private information.
- Repeatedly derailing discussions after a maintainer asks to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, or block participation
when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
